RESEARCH STREAMS:

1. Video memorability prediction
   - Segment-aware multimodal framework: predicts which video moments stick in memory
   - 5%+ improvement in Spearman rank correlation on LAMBDA benchmark over whole-video baselines
   - Luxury brand case study: creatives with highest predicted memorability got 76% higher CTR
   - Techniques: temporal binning, rank-aware supervision, residual fusion, shared multimodal encoder

2. Self-improving LLM agents (EDRA)
   - Doctoral research targeting MM '26: Experience-Driven Rule Adaptation for LLM agents
   - Episodic memory with temporal alignment, HDBSCAN clustering, LLM-driven rule induction, consistency-triggered revision
   - Proposed SOMIN-Drift benchmark from real longitudinal marketing data
   - Addresses a gap in TRAD, MetaFlowLLM, NeSyPR: none does online rule revision under domain drift

3. Influencer marketing automation (ToucHire)
   - SIGIR '26 Industrial Talk: multi-agent system for end-to-end influencer marketing
   - 9 agents on Model Context Protocol (MCP) with shared blackboard
   - 200,000+ influencer profiles across Instagram, YouTube, TikTok
   - Deployed numbers: 85% reduction in time-to-first-outreach, 54% increase in creator response rates, 12x campaign capacity per marketer, 68% reduction in contract completion time
   - Techniques: dense semantic search, HNSW vector indexing, 768-dim marketing-tuned embeddings, RAG-driven outreach

4. Professional content generation (SOINSPIRE)
   - SIGIR '26 Demo Track: agentic marketing ideation platform
   - Dynamic RAG (DRAG) grounded in near-real-time LinkedIn thought-leadership corpus
   - Content scoring: nDCG@10 = 0.829 vs. ChatGPT (0.750) and Gemini (0.769)
   - Decomposes successful content into personas, narrative tensions, relevance signals, strategic themes
   - Autonomous pipeline via Zapier for daily LinkedIn publishing

5. Privacy-compliant contextual advertising
   - SIGIR '26 Doctoral Consortium: dual-stream multimodal retrieval for cookie-less video ad placement
   - BERTopic on visual keyframes (CLIP-ViT) and audio transcripts (multilingual MiniLM)
   - GDPR/PIPEDA compliant — no behavioral tracking
   - Goal: real-time in-stream Dynamic Creative Optimization (DCO)

6. Marketing strategy co-creation
