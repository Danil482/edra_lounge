# Current archetypes model academic researchers not agencies

The EDRA preference matrix, drift events, and synthetic visitor pool are built around academic archetypes: PhD students, postdocs, professors. The ASK_SIZE values are `co-author`, `intro`, `trial` — academic collaboration language.

## The mismatch

The real Defy is [[Defy sells Monitor Automate Report to creative agencies|AI-SaaS for creative agencies]], not an academic research group. At a conference booth, a visitor who checks DEFY.group on LinkedIn will see agency SaaS — not research collaboration. This creates a credibility gap.

## Impact on the demo

- Synthetic mode: works fine (visitors are simulated researchers, everything is internally consistent)
- Live mode: the agent talks about "research collaboration" to someone whose LinkedIn says "Creative Director at Ogilvy" — awkward

## Resolution

See [[Hybrid path C balances Defy facts with research vocab]] — the recommended approach keeps synthetic archetypes but rewrites prompts with real Defy facts and adds refusal behaviour for out-of-ICP visitors.
