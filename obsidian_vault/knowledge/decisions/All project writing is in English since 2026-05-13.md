# All project writing is in English since 2026-05-13

**Decision**: all new writing — code, comments, commits, Obsidian notes, CLAUDE.md, README, TASK.md, dashboards, reports — must be in English. User-facing chat in Russian is fine (that is conversation, not artifact).

**Why**: the project crosses language boundaries (Russian-speaking user, English-speaking founders, international research audience). English is the common denominator for all written artifacts.

**Migration**: on 2026-05-13, all existing Obsidian session notes were translated from Russian to English. The rule was codified in `CLAUDE.md`.

**Exception**: preserve original quotations from third-party sources in their source language.
